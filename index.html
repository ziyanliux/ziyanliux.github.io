Joint Ph.D. Student · USTC × Shanghai AI Laboratory

I am currently a joint Ph.D. student at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) and Shanghai AI Laboratory. My M.S. advisor is Academician Feng Wu, and my Ph.D. advisors are Prof. Dahua Lin and Prof. Feng Zhao. I received my Bachelor's degree in 2024 from the School of the Gifted Young at USTC. My current research focuses on LLM post-training and Agentic AI. My research interests also include Embodied AI, especially World Models and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models. A recurring theme across my work is improving the efficiency of large-scale AI systems. Previously, I worked on AI4EDA, mainly reinforcement learning for chip placement optimization.
